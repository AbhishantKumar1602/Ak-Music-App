YouTube Creator Tips

Choosing the Right Music

Match your music to your content's energy and target audience. Upbeat tracks work for vlogs, while ambient music suits tutorials.

Audio Levels

Keep background music 15-20dB lower than your voice. Music should enhance, not compete with your narration.

Track Duration

Use shorter loops for intros/outros (15-30 seconds) and longer tracks for background music throughout your video.

Viewer Retention

Good background music can increase viewer retention by 20-30%. Choose tracks that complement your content style.
